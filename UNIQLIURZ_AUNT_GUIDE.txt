=== TASK 1: SHOPIFY PAYMENTS (MOST IMPORTANT) ===
Purpose: So customers can actually buy products (Buy Now button)

1. Open Google Chrome
2. Go to: https://admin.shopify.com/store/uniqliurz
3. Click Settings (bottom left)
4. Click "Payments"
5. Click "Add payment provider" or "Complete setup"
6. Choose "Shopify Payments"
7. Fill in:
   - Business type: Sole proprietorship or Individual
   - Legal business name: Uniqliurz (or your name)
   - Address: Your New Jersey address
   - EIN or SSN: Your SSN (it's safe, Shopify is trusted)
   - Product type: Handmade goods / Crafts
8. Click "Complete setup"
9. Done! Now "Buy Now" on www.uniqliurz.com will work

=== TASK 2: FIX ROOT DOMAIN (uniqliurz.com without www) ===
Purpose: So uniqliurz.com (no www) also shows the website

1. Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com
2. Search for "Cloud DNS" in the top search bar
3. Click on "uniqliurz.com" zone
4. Look for an A record with blank name (called "@")
5. If it shows 23.227.38.65 (Shopify's IP), change it to 76.76.21.21 (Vercel's IP)
6. If no A record exists, click "Add Record Set" and fill:
   - DNS Name: leave blank
   - Type: A
   - TTL: 300
   - IPv4 Address: 76.76.21.21
7. Click Create or Save
8. Wait 10-30 minutes, then visit uniqliurz.com to check

SECTION 1: SHOPIFY PAYMENTS (MAKE BUY NOW WORK)
-------------------------------------------------
The "Buy Now" button on product pages redirects to Shopify checkout.
For it to work, you need to set up payment + shipping in Shopify.

Step 1: Payments
  - Go to Shopify admin > Settings > Payments
  - Choose a provider:
    * Shopify Payments (recommended - 2.9%+30c per transaction, no monthly fee)
    * PayPal (alternative)
  - Enter your business details (bank account, tax info, etc.)
  - Save

Step 2: Shipping
  - Go to Settings > Shipping and delivery
  - Add shipping zones and rates (US only, or international)
  - Suggested: Free shipping over $75, flat rate $7.99 under $75

Step 3: Taxes
  - Go to Settings > Taxes and duties
  - Enable automatic tax calculation (Shopify handles US sales tax)
  - This is critical for legal compliance in New Jersey

Once all three are set up, "Buy Now" will work seamlessly.
Customers click > go to Shopify checkout > pay > you get the order.


SECTION 2: DOMAIN SETUP (www.uniqliurz.com -> VERCEL)
-------------------------------------------------------
Currently uniqliurz.com points to Shopify (your live store).
This keeps working - no changes to your live store.

To show the beautiful custom site on www.uniqliurz.com:

Step 1: Already done - Vercel has www.uniqliurz.com added as a domain

Step 2: Add this DNS record at your domain registrar:
  Type: CNAME
  Name: www
  Value/Points to: cname.vercel-dns.com

This sends www.uniqliurz.com traffic to Vercel while uniqliurz.com stays on Shopify.

Result:
  - uniqliurz.com -> Shopify (your admin/store)
  - www.uniqliurz.com -> Our custom site (beautiful frontend)
  - "Buy Now" goes to Shopify checkout (hosted on Shopify's domain)


SECTION 3: ADDING/EDITING PRODUCTS
------------------------------------
All product management happens in Shopify admin:

1. Go to admin.shopify.com/store/uniqliurz
2. Click Products
3. Add new: Click "Add product" > fill title, description, price, images
4. Edit existing: Click any product > edit fields
5. For categories: Add a tag matching one of:
   resin-art, engraved, frames, bottles, keychains, custom

The website automatically pulls products from Shopify.
No code changes needed when you add/edit/remove products.
